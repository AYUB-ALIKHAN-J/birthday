Happy Birthday, Devaranjanaa
I’ve been sitting here thinking about everything we’ve been through to get to this point. It’s been 1.5 years, but looking back, it honestly feels like a lifetime of memories packed into that time.

The Beginning
I still remember the first time I saw you at that college quiz in our first year. It was November 14th, and Vivek was running the show. You were so quiet and didn’t say much, but I just thought you were the cutest person in the room. I was honestly a bit starstruck—I had no clue what to say to you, so I just admired you from a distance for that whole first semester, way too shy to even try to start a real conversation.

The Shift
Everything changed second semester when we finally connected on social media. I felt like I finally had a chance, but I was still so nervous to do anything about it. Then came that New Year’s phone call. I can’t even remember if it was you or your friend who called, but I just remember how happy I was to hear your voice. After that, we started talking about notes and studies, and for the first time, I felt totally comfortable just talking to a girl. That was the moment I realized I wasn’t just interested—I was really falling for you.

The Moments I Keep
I was so terrified of losing you as a friend that I kept my feelings to myself for a long time. But I couldn’t ignore the signs. I still have that band you tied on my arm at the IT block Halloween party. I’ve kept it all this time because that evening meant the world to me. Even back then, I’d get so jealous when you talked to other guys, even though I tried my absolute best to play it cool.

The Risk at "High For This"
Proposing to you at the High For This cafe was one of the hardest things I’ve ever done. Deep down, I already knew you were going to say no, but I couldn't live with the "what ifs." Everything happened exactly as I expected, but even after the rejection and that month of awkward silence during the break, you were the one who reached out. You brought us back together, and my feelings for you only got stronger from there.

The Long Five Months
Then we hit that rough patch after the function at Anuz’s. I let my jealousy get the best of me—that was my mistake—and when you told me not to talk to you anymore, it felt like I’d lost a part of myself. Those five months were the longest of my life. I wanted to talk to you every single day, but I was just so scared of how you’d react. Then, August 3rd happened. That phone call where you asked, "How long are we going to be like this?" was the best thing that ever happened to me.

The Stairs
I’ll never forget that evening on the stairs of the block. When you finally said you loved me too, it felt like everything in the world finally clicked. Holding your hand for the first time that day... I don’t think I’ve ever been happier in my life.

Where We Are Now
We’ve survived the stress of searching for internships and the whole transition into "adulting" together. I even heard somewhere that people gain weight when they’re in a happy, healthy relationship—and looking at me now, I guess that’s definitely true!

Every moment feels special when I’m with you. Even just thinking about our night walks at Gandhipuram gives me so much peace. I’m so grateful for every high and low we’ve been through. I love you more than I did yesterday, and I really hope I get to celebrate every single one of your birthdays by your side.

Happy Birthday!
